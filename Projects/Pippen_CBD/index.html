Information about Pippen Collab

The CBD collab, also known as the Pippen collab, was hosted last year in September during the 20th to the 29th. It focused on the Central area of Singapore, mainly Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the bay and most of the rest of Central Business District. This event is held to commemorate the first six months of Singapore BTE
